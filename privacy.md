# TaliCompanion Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 23, 2026_

TaliCompanion is a Chrome extension and companion iOS app that records
Flesh and Blood games played on [Talishar.net](https://talishar.net) so
you can review them later. This page describes what we collect, why, and
how you can remove it.

Questions or deletion requests: **talicompanion.support@gmail.com**.

## 1. Data we collect

**Account information.** When you sign in, Google Sign-In shares your
email address, name, profile picture, and a Google account identifier.
We use this to create and identify your TaliCompanion account. We never
see your Google password.

**Talishar identity.** While you are signed in to both TaliCompanion and
Talishar in the same browser, the extension reads your Talishar handle
from Talishar so that games you save are tagged with your verified
in-game name and so we can match you against other TaliCompanion users.

**Game data.** When you click **Save** on a completed game, we upload a
detailed record of the match to your account. This includes both
players' Talishar handles, the heroes played, the result, and a
turn-by-turn record of the cards you played, the damage dealt and taken,
and the actions you took during the game. We do **not** record your
opponent's hand or arsenal contents. Games you do not save are kept only
in your browser and cleared when Chrome closes.

**Profile data.** Any username you choose, your rating and rating
history, your per-hero win/loss totals, and any friends you add on
TaliCompanion.

## 2. What we do not collect

- We do not log your IP address, browsing history, or activity on any
  site other than talishar.net.
- We do not collect payment information. TaliCompanion is free.
- We do not run advertising or marketing analytics.
- We do not train machine-learning models on your data.
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own
  use.

## 3. Where data is stored

TaliCompanion data is stored on Google Cloud using Firebase. Google
processes data on our behalf as our cloud provider. See
<https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>.

## 4. Who can see your data

- **You** can see all of your own data in the TaliCompanion iOS app.
- **Other signed-in TaliCompanion users** can see your TaliCompanion
  username, Talishar handle, display name, profile picture, and current
  rating so they can add you as a friend or view your rating as their
  opponent.
- **Opponents** in games you save cannot see those saves. Your saved
  games live under your account only.
- **We** (the TaliCompanion maintainers) have administrative access for
  debugging and abuse response and do not read your game data except
  when investigating a specific report.

## 5. Third parties

TaliCompanion uses Google / Firebase for authentication and data
storage, and reads Talishar's own public login endpoint to learn your
Talishar handle. The extension does not send any TaliCompanion data to
Talishar or to any other third party.

## 6. Your rights

- **View your data** — everything saved to your account is visible in
  the TaliCompanion iOS app.
- **Delete a game** — discard in the extension popup before saving, or
  delete it in the iOS app after saving.
- **Reset your stats** — Settings → Reset All Stats in the iOS app
  deletes every saved game, win/loss record, and rating.
- **Delete your account** — email **talicompanion.support@gmail.com**
  and we will delete your account and all associated data within 30
  days.

## 7. Retention

We keep your data until you delete it or request account deletion.
Unsaved game state in your browser is cleared when the browser closes.

## 8. Children

TaliCompanion is not directed to children under 13, and we do not
knowingly collect data from them.

## 9. Changes to this policy

If we change what we collect or how we use it, we will update this page
and note the change in the Chrome Web Store listing.

## 10. Contact

Questions, access requests, or deletion requests:
**talicompanion.support@gmail.com**.
